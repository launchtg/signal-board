# SAMPLE_VALIDATION_CASE.md

This is a sample of what a completed Validation-stage review should look like.

---

## Project
- **Name:** AISearchRank
- **Stage at start:** Design
- **Validation window:** 14 days
- **Goal:** Determine whether marketers/SEO operators want AI search visibility tracking enough to join a waitlist or reply with intent

## Hypothesis

If we present AISearchRank as an affordable way to track brand mentions across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews, then marketers and SEO operators frustrated by blind AI visibility will sign up for early access.

## Validation Asset
- **Landing page URL:** `https://aisearchrank.com`
- **CTA:** `Get early access`
- **Offer:** lightweight AI visibility monitoring for brands

## Distribution
Traffic sources tested:
- Reddit posts in relevant SEO/marketing communities
- direct outreach to likely operators/agencies
- social posts discussing AI search visibility

## Results Summary
- **Visitors:** 312
- **Signups:** 29
- **Qualified:** 12
- **Conversion:** 9.3%
- **High-intent replies:** 4
- **Pilot/demo interest:** 2

## Signal Quality

### What was encouraging
- the right audience responded
- multiple people already had a manual workflow for checking AI mentions
- pricing sensitivity existed, but the pain felt real
- interest was strongest from agencies and SaaS marketers

### What was weak
- some curiosity traffic was low-intent
- not everyone understood the difference between AI search visibility and traditional SEO at first glance

## Interpretation

This validation result would generally be considered **promising** because:
- conversion is respectable for cold validation traffic
- qualified signups are meaningful
- responses came from the intended audience
- there was some concrete pilot/demo interest

## Decision
- **Outcome:** Move to Build
- **Why:** enough signal exists to justify implementing the MVP
