The problem

New businesses are one of the cleanest buying signals in local and SMB outreach.

  • Insurance agents need to quote new businesses before another broker does.
  • Accountants and bookkeepers want fresh business owners before they already have a provider.
  • Payroll, payments, and B2B sales teams need a faster path to high-intent prospects.

Right now, the workflow is still ugly: check state websites, click around broken public portals, copy names into a spreadsheet, then manually start enrichment and outreach.

What teams do now

  • Open state filing portals one by one
  • Filter manually by date, type, or county
  • Copy names into spreadsheets
  • Miss filings when nobody checks that day
  • Buy stale lead lists later at enterprise prices

What NewFilings does

  • Watch target states automatically
  • Filter by business type, keyword, and geography
  • Send fresh alert summaries
  • Give you a prioritized queue instead of raw noise
  • Help you reach new businesses first

How it works

1. Set your watchlist

Choose states, business types, geographies, and keywords that match your ideal customer profile.

2. We monitor new filings

NewFilings checks public filing sources and looks for matches that fit your watchlist.

3. You get the lead faster

Instead of checking government websites manually, you get a usable alert workflow built around speed.
